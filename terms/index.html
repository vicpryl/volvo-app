XC90_Telemetry_Logger — Terms and Conditions

Last updated: 2026-05-25

1. Scope

XC90_Telemetry_Logger (the "Application") is a personal, non-commercial command-line tool developed and used by a single individual (the "User") to access read-only telemetry, energy state, and GPS location of a single, privately-owned Volvo vehicle linked to the User's own Volvo ID account.

2. No third parties

The Application is operated solely by, and for the benefit of, the User. It is not offered, distributed, sold or licensed to any other natural or legal person. No data obtained through the Application is shared with, transmitted to, or processed by any third party.

3. Data handling

All telemetry data and OAuth credentials retrieved through the Volvo Cars APIs are stored locally on the User's personal computing device, with file permissions restricted to the User's account (0600 / owner-only). Data is used exclusively for the User's personal analysis of their own vehicle. No data is uploaded to any external service.
